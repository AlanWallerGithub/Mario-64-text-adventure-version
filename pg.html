Princess Chapel room
This room is very nice and small. In it there are paintings. Peach is praying! She’s got her hands on her chest! Protection. Her heart. It’s protected. It’s a small room because it seems to be like a small little religious space. Princess Peach is praying, or at least holding her hands. Her chest is being protected. The glass windows are colorful, but they hide a drop just behind them. If you fall down them, you fall down for a long time. It’s something beautiful.
